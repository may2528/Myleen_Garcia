Portfolio

Layered Mid-length Cut

Textured Layers are characterised by varied lengths, texture, and volume, offering a chic, effortless style.

Special Fashion Makeup

Special makeup fashion combines artistic makeup techniques with high-fashion aesthetics, creating bold, avant-garde looks. It often involves exaggerated colors, dramatic contours, and innovative textures to highlight or transform facial features.

Hair and Bridal Makeup Looks

Hair and bridal makeup looks are designed to enhance the natural beauty of the bride with timeless elegance and sophistication. Bridal makeup emphasizes a radiant, long-lasting glow, with soft, flattering tones that complement the bride’s features and personal style.

Food Inspired Creative Hair and Makeup

Food-inspired creative hair and makeup combines culinary themes with beauty artistry, resulting in playful and imaginative looks. Using colors, textures, and shapes reminiscent of foods like fruits, desserts, artists create bold, whimsical designs that transform the face and hair into edible-inspired art.

Medium length haircut with waves and Simple Makeup

A medium-length haircut with waves and simple makeup creates an effortlessly chic and natural look. The haircut, typically falling around shoulder length, features soft, loose waves that add volume and movement, enhancing the hair’s texture.

Wavy Bridal Hair and Makeup

Wavy bridal hair and makeup offers a romantic, soft look that enhances natural beauty with elegance. The hair, styled in loose, flowing waves, frames the face beautifully, adding volume and a timeless touch to the bridal look.

Runway Hair and Makeup

Runway hair and makeup are bold, high-impact styles crafted to make a statement and complement designer fashion on the catwalk. Hair may range from sleek and polished to extravagant and voluminous, often styled in unique shapes or with eye-catching accessories.

Dark Brown Eyes and Hair Creative Makeup

Creative makeup for dark brown eyes and hair emphasizes rich, contrasting colors and bold designs to enhance the depth and warmth of these features.
